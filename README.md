### VCDuration

Shows how much time has passed since you joined a voice channel.

Works for more than 24 hrs unlike old powercord and better discord versions.

Direct Download Link: [dev.tharki.VCDuration.asar](https://github.com/YofukashiNo/VCDuration/releases/latest/download/dev.tharki.VCDuration.asar)
